Bianca Trinkenreich

Researcher

I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at Oregon State University, researching about Human Factors of Software Engineering. My research interests include Diversity, Equity, Inclusion and Belonging, Open Source Software, DevOps, Teams Sustainability, Developer Productivity, Developer Experience, Computer-Supported Cooperative Work, OKRs, maturity models, IT services management processes. I have first-authored more than 25 papers in top venues of my area, including ICSE, ESEM, CSCW, IST, TSE, and TOSEM, and had received awards at ICSE Technical Track 2023 (Distinguished Paper Award), ICSE SEIS 2022 (Best Paper Award), CSCW 2020 (Honored Mention Award), Software Quality Brazilian Conference - SBQS 2018 (Best master’s Degree Thesis Award), Product-Focused Software Process Improvement - PROFES 2015 (Indication for Best Paper Award), Annual Workshop for Software and Services Quality Improvement - WAMPS 2014 (Best Paper Award). I was also awarded an Outstanding Graduate Student 2021 by Northern Arizona University. Work authorization: USA (EB2-NIW visa), EU (citizen), Brazil (citizen)
